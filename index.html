Scoring & Round System

Each level consists of five rounds.
The winner of each round is awarded 2 points.
If the round ends in a draw, both players are awarded 1 point.
(You can check the scores at the Pause Menu)

Difficulty Levels

  • Easy

    • In easy mode, you get the first move. Computer plays like a dummy.
    I've never seen any idiot who lost in this difficulty.

  • Medium

    • Computer gets the first move, is a bit wiser, blocks your moves, and plays with some sense.

  • Hard

    • Computer also gets first move and goes all out. Good luck trying to win here
